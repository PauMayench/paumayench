Welcome to my GitHub! Here you'll find projects, experiments, and small ideas I build while learning and exploring different areas of computer science and quantum technologies.

I'm a Computer Engineer, currently studying Quantum Information Science and Technology at TU Delft. I enjoy coding a lot, especially when it involves solving interesting problems or learning something new along the way.

I'm particularly interested in optimization problems, quantum machine learning, quantum mapping and scheduling, and logic programming. I also like coding challenges, algorithms, and projects that push me to think in new ways.

Most of the things here are part of my learning process: some are university-related, some are personal projects, and some are just ideas I wanted to try out.
